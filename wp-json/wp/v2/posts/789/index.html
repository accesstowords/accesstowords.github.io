The cost of a BSL interpreter can vary depending on several factors, but here’s a breakdown to give you an idea:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    \n
  • Hourly Rate:<\/strong> Professional, qualified BSL interpreter rates typically range from \u00a330 to \u00a350 per hour.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Minimum Charge:<\/strong> Many agencies have a minimum booking fee, often covering a set number of hours (e.g., 3 hours) regardless of the total event duration. This covers the interpreter’s time and preparation for the assignment.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Travel Expenses:<\/strong> If the interpreter needs to travel a significant distance to the assignment location, travel costs may be added on top of the hourly rate.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Additional Services:<\/strong> Some interpreters may offer additional services like pre-assignment preparation or equipment rental, which would incur additional charges.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    Here are some resources to help you get a more accurate quote for your specific needs:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

      \n
    • National Union of British Sign Language Interpreters (NUBSLI): <\/strong>https:\/\/www.nubsli.com\/guidance\/interpreter-fees\/<\/a> provides guidance on interpreter fees.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    • Local BSL interpreter agencies:<\/strong> Searching online for BSL interpreter agencies in your area can give you quotes based on their specific rates and service offerings.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

      Keep in mind that sign language interpretation is widely considered to be just about the most cognitively intense profession there is, with interpreters typically working 20 minutes at a time, followed by much-needed break. For long sessions, such as conferences, you will probably want to double-up on interpreters.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

      By understanding these factors, you can get a better idea of the cost involved in hiring a BSL interpreter for your needs.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
