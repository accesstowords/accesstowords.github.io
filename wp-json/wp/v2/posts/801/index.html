TV and Film:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

    \n
  • Sophie Stone:<\/strong> The first deaf person to win a place at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts (RADA) and the first deaf actress to appear on Doctor Who. She’s a prominent figure advocating for deaf representation in the acting world.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Rose Ayling-Ellis:<\/strong> Rose is a talented British actress and trailblazer, known for her groundbreaking role as the first deaf contestant on “Strictly Come Dancing.” With her infectious energy and remarkable dance moves, she captivates audiences while breaking barriers and inspiring inclusivity in the entertainment world.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Tasha Stones:<\/strong> Tasha Stones is an inspirational figure in the culinary world, known for her exceptional talent on the Great British Bake Off as its first deaf contestant, and for her vibrant advocacy that has endeared her to fans and fellow bakers alike.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    Athletes:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

      \n
    • Victoria Westwood:<\/strong> A British Paralympic swimmer who has won multiple gold medals at the Deaflympics. She’s a motivational speaker and advocate for Deaf sports and inclusion.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

      Presenters and Personalities:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

        \n
      • Becky Mingins<\/a>:<\/strong> By day, Becky is a swimming teacher living in Brighton.\u00a0 But outside the school day, she posts funny and educational videos on TikTok and Instagram in British Sign Language, and she possibly the most-loved Deaf influencer in the United Kingdom with a cult following far greater than the number of people who use BSL!<\/li>\n\n\n\n
      • Lucy Edwards:<\/strong> A TV presenter and BSL interpreter who works on various programs aimed at the Deaf community. She is known for her bubbly personality and commitment to Deaf accessibility.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
      • Scott Garthwaite:<\/strong> The Punk Chef is a professional Chef and a TV Chef Presenter. He has appeared in several TV cooking shows showcasing sign language with food which has made him one of the first celebrity Deaf chef to emerge in mainstream television.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
      • Sign language presenters on the BSL Zone:<\/strong> The BSL Zone is a British television channel featuring programs entirely in BSL. Presenters on this channel showcase BSL fluency and contribute to Deaf entertainment and news.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n
